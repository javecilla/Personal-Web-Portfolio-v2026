Amid the electrifying atmosphere of the IT Congress Side Events, our team dove headfirst into the Hackathon, ready to tackle real-world challenges. Fueled by creativity, caffeine, and pure determination, we coded our way to victory! Our challenge? Develop an innovative eCommerce website from scratch within the given time limit.
<br/><br/>
Competing before a prestigious panel of judges including <strong>Jean Eren Fajardo</strong> from <a href="https://www.youtube.com/c/sdptsolutions" target="_blank"><strong>SDPT Solutions</strong></a>—a renowned YouTube programming educator known for providing free programming tutorials and technical education in Tagalog to the Filipino developer community—we demonstrated seamless UI and UX logic with a user-friendly shopping experience. The judges were impressed with our innovative approach and technical execution, awarding us the championship title. This victory showcased our expertise in front-end development, problem-solving, and teamwork, proving that pressure is just fuel for innovation!
